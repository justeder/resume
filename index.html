About

I am an active global evangelist for investment and entrepreneurship.

Mentor and angel investor of tech startups. I invest in the financial markets since 2020.

I have co-founded several companies: a bar of drinks to go (2006), a mexican food restaurant (2016), a custom mobile bar (since the early days in 2007 all the way to the reborn in 2016), a bicycle advertising agency (2017), a delivery service (2018), a burguer (2018), and a coaching to start a business from 0 (2020). I started Visionaty.com in 2020 as my entrepreneur education initiative to train and support Latin America's new generation of entrepreneurs. I am also the author of the book “Cómo crear tu propio negocio en 20 pasos” (2018).
